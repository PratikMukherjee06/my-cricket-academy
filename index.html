Our Story: Building Tomorrow's Cricketers

Founded in 1995, Sodepur Club has been a cornerstone of cricket excellence in the region. Our Academy was established with a clear vision: to nurture raw talent, instill professional discipline, and create a clear pathway for young cricketers to achieve state and national recognition. We believe that success is built on a foundation of technique, physical conditioning, and mental resilience.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, high-quality cricket coaching in a supportive and challenging environment, fostering the next generation of sporting leaders and skilled players.
